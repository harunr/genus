Share your story.

We want to transform how the public understands Palaeoscience. To deepen knowledge of the human experience by exploring exciting, novel, thought-provoking, and unconventional ideas. And you can help.

South Africa has an unmatched geographical advantage with a fossil collection that will ignite even the wildest imaginations. So where better to uncover the origins of life than right at our very feet? From fossils to the future, Palaeoscience explores what life was like in centuries past and where we might be headed. How does human evolution impact the way we communicate and live today? How can heritage inform us about law and ethics? How can ancient organisms help us understand climate change and current extinction events? At GENUS, we aim to answer all of these questions and more.

To do this, we publish written and multimedia pieces that provide smart and surprising insights into evolution, climate change, biodiversity, human evolution, heritage management, curations, history, and current events with a palaeo twist. Our goal is to reach the general public who have little or no background in the field. So we work to ensure that our pieces are compelling, accurate and grounded in great storytelling.

We welcome pitches from scientists and journalists. All submissions must be original.
Each pitch is considered by the GENUS team.

What we strive for in our writing:

  • Dig deep to find groundbreaking insights and unique perspectives
  • Ignite wonder and spark curiosity through engaging and relevant storytelling
  • Encourage diversity and ensure that writers from every background have a voice
  • Write with respect and integrity for the benefit of our readers and subject matter
  • Make academic insights accessible and intriguing to the public
  • Help Palaeoscientists become engaging storytellers through quality writing
Send your story ideas to info.genus@wits.ac.za
